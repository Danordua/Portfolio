I am a Product Designer with experience in the entire design process: encompassing user research, problem definition, ideation, validation and measurement. My expertise lies in unraveling complex problems through a human-centered approach, understanding the user problems and needs to ideate and validate solutions. Also, I have a strong focus validating the product-market fit, ensuring that the solutions align with the needs and expectations of the market.

“I help businesses striking a balance between their objectives and their users needs to achieve a successful digital product.”

My journey.

I started as a generalist, being the sole designer at a startup. As the application grew, I quickly became the leader and founder of a design team, gaining valuable experience in defining design strategy. I learned how to collaborate with the stakeholders to balance the user needs with the business objectives and ultimately creating a sustainable and feasible designs for the development team.

How can I help you:

  • Generate a product strategy through research and real user data.
  • Discover, evaluate and validate your product-market fit.
  • Establishing key metrics to measure and track product performance and user experience while aligning them with the overarching business objectives.
  • Bringing your idea to life!
