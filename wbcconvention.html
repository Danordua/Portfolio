The World Boxing Council (WBC) hosts the WBC Annual Convention each year, bringing together fighters, trainers, promoters, and fans to celebrate the sport they love. The event covers various topics, like safety measures and rule updates, all aimed at shaping the future of boxing. A key moment of the convention is the registration process, where fans and athletes secure their spots to join the fun.

My role on this project was as a Product Designer. My goal was to create a seamless registration experience, updated with industry standards, and easily accessible to everyone, while ensuring the quality and brand identity of the World Boxing Council.

What I learned the most during this project:

  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing
  • Prototyping
  • UI Design
